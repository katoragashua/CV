Ronaldinho

The best i've seen...

Ronaldinho is unarguably one of the best players to ever kick a ball. His skills were electrifying, his touch; impeccable, his vision; ineffable and his smile was more than charming. He dazzled defenders all around the globe and is one of the most loved players ever. The man was pure magic...

Life + Times

  • 1980- Born Ronaldo de Assis Moreira on 21 March, 1980 in the city of Porto Alegre, Brazil.
  • 1987- Started his youth football career at Gremio where his elder brother Roberto played.
  • 1998- Started his senior football career at Gremio.
  • 1999- Ronaldinho began his senior national team career with the Brazil National Team, winning the Confederations Cup the same year.
  • 2001- Ronaldinho made his first move to Europe with a move to Paris Saint Germain.
  • 2002- Won the FIFA World Cup with the Brazil national team. He scored a wonderful freekick against England in the process. He was just electric.
  • 2003- Made a bold and big move to Barcelona CF, swapping France for Spain.
  • 2005- Won another Confederations Cup with the national team and a Balon d'Or award.
  • 2006- Won the UEFA Champions League trophy with Barcelona.
  • 2008- Moved to AC-Milan where he endured a less successful spell, albeit he was very good. He won the Scudetto.
  • 2011- Moved to CR Flamengo in his native Brazil.
  • 2012- Switched Flamengo for Atletico Mineiro where he had another very successful spell, winning the Copa Libertadores and the South American Player of the Year award.
  • 2013- Retired from the Brazil National Team.
  • 2014- Moved to Queretaro in Mexico.
  • 2015-Fluminense; this is where he ended his professional career.
  • Today- Ronaldinho Gaucho is still very alive and showing the world his electric skill whenever he's called upon for charity matches. He has never lost his smile.
